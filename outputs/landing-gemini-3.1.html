Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own clubs?
Not at all! We provide complimentary, premium men's and women's club rentals at every bay. However, if you prefer to use your own, you are more than welcome to bring your bag.
How many people fit in one bay?
Each simulator bay comfortably fits up to 6 guests. If you have a larger party, we recommend booking adjacent bays or contacting us for a group event package.
Are kids allowed at Fairway Social?
Yes! We are a family-friendly establishment during the day. After 8:00 PM, Fairway Social becomes a 21+ venue. Valid ID is required for entry after 8:00 PM.
Can I host a private event here?
Absolutely. We offer everything from reserved multi-bay sections to full venue buyouts. Our event team will work with you on custom catering and drink packages. Reach out via our contact page for more info.
