01 — What's inside

A bar first, a simulator second.

We built the room the other way around from most simulator places. The bar comes first. The tech is real — but you're not sitting in a Best Buy.

— 01

TrackMan 4, four bays

Each bay runs the same dual-radar system the PGA uses. St Andrews, Pebble Beach, Torrey Pines — 150+ courses, plus the driving range mode for working on something specific. Club data and ball flight are within a yard of outdoor reality, which is either reassuring or humbling depending on your swing.

— 02

The bar

Twelve taps, mostly local. Trillium, Night Shift, Dorchester Brewing. Full liquor, no novelty cocktails. Bar seats fourteen, plus four high-tops in the back.

— 03

Kitchen

Sandwiches, fries, wings, a rotating special. Open until close, Wednesday through Sunday.

— 04

Leagues

Eight-week seasons, Tuesday through Sunday nights. Handicapped, so an 18 can play a 9. Next season starts in January.

— 05

Private bookings

Whole-venue rentals Thursday and Sunday nights. Up to 60 people. Good for birthdays, work outings, the league you actually want to win.

02 — Rates

Per bay, per hour. Not per person.

Up to four players per bay at no extra charge. Six is doable but you'll be sharing swings. All rates include the simulator, the data, and table service from the bar.
