Good Questions

Before you tee it up

Not even a little. About half our guests have never played a real round. The simulators have forgiving casual modes, mini-games, and auto-gimmes, and our bay hosts will happily give you a two-minute crash course. If you can bowl, you can do this.

Each bay comfortably seats six with couches, a table, and its own service. Pricing is per bay, not per person: $35/hr off-peak (weekdays before 5pm) and $55/hr prime (evenings and weekends). Six friends splitting prime time works out to about $9 each per hour.

Life happens. Cancel or reschedule free up to 24 hours before your tee time — just reply to your confirmation email or call us. Inside 24 hours we charge one hour of bay time, and no-shows are charged the full reservation. League dues pause anytime with a week's notice.
