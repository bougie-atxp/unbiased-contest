Questions?

What’s the minimum age?

21+ after 8pm. All ages welcome before 8pm with adult supervision.

Can I bring my own clubs?

Absolutely. We also have a full set of rental clubs available at no extra charge.

How far in advance should I book?

Weekends fill up quickly. We recommend booking at least 3–5 days ahead.

Do you host private events?

Yes. We have packages for groups of 8–40. Reach out via the booking form or email us.
